Scott Skow - Employers, Interviews

Remember that the interview is not just about interviewing you; you are interviewing them as well.

Standards for choosing your employer:

V - Values - You really want your values to line up with the company's values.
I - Incentive - What motivates you to work for them?
C - Culture - Google's motto: Don't be evil
E - Environment - Natural light, open, short commute, etc.
S - Structure - How many higher-ups/bosses? Where do you fit in the structure?
